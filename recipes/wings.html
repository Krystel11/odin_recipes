Garlic Chicken Wings

Description

Prepare delicious garlic chicken wings in just a few minutes with this practical recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 Pounds chicken wings
  • 1/2 Teaspoon oregano
  • 3 Cloves of minced garlic
  • 1 Tablespoon cornstarch
  • 2 Tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 Tablespoon of butter
  • 1/4 Cup of water

Steps

  1. In a bowl, wash the wings and cut the tips. Add the oregano, cornstarch, garlic, oil and marinate for 5 minutes.
  2. In a frying pan over medium heat, place the butter, water, and wings and cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Reduce heat to medium, cover the pan and let cook for 15 more minutes.
  4. Serve and enjoy!
